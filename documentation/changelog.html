Do not forget to backup your files and read the changelog before updating your Front copy. If you come across with any problems with Front template during the update and development processes, feel free to contact our support group at htmlstream.com/contact-us and our team will assist you within a short time.

Migrating to v2

Front Dashboard v2.0 is a major rewrite of the entire template. The most notable changes are summarized below, followed by more specific changes to relevant components. Hence, below are the breaking changes to bear in mind when moving from v1.x to v2.0 or any older version of Front.

The most notable improvements made in Front Dashboard v2.0

  • Bootstrap v5 - Built on top of the latest Bootstrap v5.
  • JavaScript - All HS libraries and scripts are fully re-written in vanilla JavaScript, except some third party libraries such as Datatables & Date Range Picker still comes with jQuery dependency.
  • HTML - classes and code structure optimized.
  • SCSS - all files and styled updated.
  • Headers (Navbars) - completely rewritten from scratch.
